What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They are used to make websites function efficiently, provide a better browsing experience, and help website owners understand how visitors interact with their site.

How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to improve your browsing experience and the functionality of our website. These cookies may be used to:

  • Remember your preferences and settings for future visits.
  • Analyze site traffic and usage patterns to enhance performance.
  • Improve website functionality, speed, and security.
  • Provide personalized content where applicable.

Managing Cookies

Most web browsers allow you to manage or disable cookies through your browser settings. You can choose to accept or reject cookies; however, please note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of some parts of our website.

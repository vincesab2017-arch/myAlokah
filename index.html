About Us

Alokah Ventures Limited, incorporated on September 26, 2022, is a fully registered logistics and supply chain provider headquartered in Kampala, Uganda. Through innovation, reliability, and professional expertise, we ensure efficient cargo movement and world-class logistics support across borders. We remain committed to service excellence, regulatory compliance, and impactful trade facilitation.
